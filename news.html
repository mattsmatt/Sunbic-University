Maps and Charts Enhance, Deepen Content of Award-Winning Senior Essays

May 26, 2022 | by Deborah Cannarella


The maps and charts that accompany this award-winning senior essay are designed to enhance the content of the essay. They provide students with additional information about the topic and help them organize their ideas. In addition, they make the essay easier to read and understand.

This map shows how the major cities of California have changed over time. Students can use this map to compare the population of each city before and after World War II. This chart illustrates the changing numbers of people who live in California's largest cities.

In this map, students can find out where the states of New York and Pennsylvania intersect. They can then use this information to determine whether these two states share a border.

Study Empowers Long COVID Patients as They Struggle to Find Relief

May 31, 2022 | by Isabella Backman


A study published in JAMA Internal Medicine has shown that patients who have been infected with COVID-19 for over two weeks are at risk of developing severe disease if they do not receive treatment.

According to the study, patients who were treated with hydroxychloroquine (HCQ) had a lower chance of getting sicker than those who did not receive HCQ.

The results of this study showed that patients who received HCQ had a lower rate of hospitalization and death compared to those who did not receive the drug.
